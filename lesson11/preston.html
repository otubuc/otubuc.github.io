Preston Idaho Music Festival

Preston music festival is a community event oriented towards live performances of singing and instrument playing that presents a theme such as musical genre (e.g., blues, folk, jazz, classical music), nationality, locality of musicians, or holiday. They are commonly held outdoors, with tents or roofed temporary stages for the performers. The event will host other attractions such as food and merchandise vending, dance, crafts, performance art, and social or cultural activities.


At the Preston music festivals may be associated with charitable causes, there will be information about social or political issues. Many of Preston's festivals are annual, or repeat at some other interval. Some, including many rock festivals, are held only once. This year's festivals are organized as for-profit concerts and others are benefits for a specific charitable cause. Another type of music festival is the educative type, organized annually in local communities, regionally, or nationally, for the benefit of amateur musicians of all ages and grades of achievement.
