Introduction

Weather warnings are important forecasts because they are used to protect life and property. Since outdoor activities are severely curtailed by heavy rain, snow and wind chill, forecasts can be used to plan activities around these events, and to plan ahead and survive them.

Site Name

The name of this weather site is "CLOUD10 Weather Center" (CWC)

Purpose & Objective

The goal of CWC site is to provide reliable weather forecast so that people and organizations can reliably use it to plan their daily and weekly outdoor activities. This site will also help them to reduce weather-related losses because they will be able to plan ahead against adverse weather conditions which will enhance societal benefits, including protection of life and property, public health and safety, and to support economic prosperity as well as the quality of life for the Preston, Soda spring and Fish Haven communities.

CWC site will combine other interesting features such as events, news, entertainment, life-style, politics, education, picture gallery, CWC online live radio, classifieds, security, reports, live traffick feeds, etc. CWC site will be implemented with responsive design techniques allowing delivery of the site and its content on all classes of devicesâ€”large-wide screens (desktop/laptop), medium-screens (tablet), and small-screens (smartphones).
