Introduction

Colors play an important role in design as well as in everyday life. The CWC website will be built using the selected color-scheme as indicated below. These combination were selected because of their mildness and weather-related brightness. There may be need to adjust the brightness or dullness of each color depending on the circumstance. I am going to be very flexible as the design progresses. I'll endeavor to be consistent in my approach and this scheme will serve as a guide.
